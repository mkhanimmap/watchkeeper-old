Risk levels

The following risk levels are applicable to iMMAP operations:
RISK LEVELS
LOW Threat is not imminent
MODERATE Threat is present but not imminent
HIGH Threat is likely
EXTREME Threat is imminent
The risk level can be used to describe the general threats for an area of operation or to describe the level of a specific threat, i.e. kidnapping of shooting.

Movement states

The following movement states are applicable to iMMAP work related travel:
MOVEMENT STATES
NORMAL No restrictions
MISSION ESSENTIAL Essential for iMMAP operations, CoP approval required; unnecessary movements are to be avoided
MISSION CRITICAL Urgent donor and senior level meetings only, CR approval required; no commuting of staff
MISSION BLACKOUT No movements
